# Carbon Emission Calculation

## Overview

The Carbon Emission Calculation skill calculates carbon emissions for factories, production processes, and products. It integrates energy consumption, raw materials, and logistics data to provide comprehensive carbon footprint analysis.

## Features

- Scope 1, 2, and 3 emissions calculation
- Direct and indirect emission sources
- Product carbon footprint (PCF) calculation
- Industry-standard emission factors
- Carbon intensity metrics
- Compliance reporting (ISO 14064, GHG Protocol)

## Input

- Energy consumption data by source type
- Raw material consumption and origins
- Logistics and transportation data
- Production output data
- Supply chain information

## Output

- Total carbon emissions (tCO2e)
- Emissions breakdown by source and scope
- Carbon intensity metrics (per unit, per revenue)
- Detailed carbon footprint report
- Compliance mapping to standards

## Use Cases

- Corporate carbon footprint reporting
- Product lifecycle carbon analysis
- Supply chain carbon accounting
- Regulatory compliance (EU ETS, carbon tax)
- Carbon offset planning
- ESG reporting

## Integration

This skill can be integrated with:
- ERP systems
- Supply chain management systems
- Environmental management systems
- Carbon accounting platforms
